editTools

editTools is a fast R package designed using compiled code meant for the anlaysis of RNA editing from whole genome sequencing and RNAseq data.

spin

spin is a R/Bash solution for easy submission of scripts on TORQUE resource managers. Designed with literate programming and organization in mind.

My talk at the 2015 Midwest ASAS conference

Abstract from my 15min presentation providing evidence of widespread RNA editing in the swine model, with results obtained using editTools. Presentation slides and audio accessible with ASAS membership. Image from whotv.com

My first publication

My first publication working at the Abkowitz Lab at the University of Washington. I performed in-vivo work to uncover the effect of FLVCR, a heme-exporter, on lymphocyte peripheral survival.
